Mr. Bear's Personal Website

Hello there! I'm Mr. Bear and this is my website. Here you can find some info about myself, my contact information, as well as some recent news about the things happening in my life. I hope you enjoy!

About me

I'm a bear. A stuffed toy teddy bear, that is! Not a real bear. I would imagine that if I were a real bear, I would be a grizzly. Grizzly bears are cool and huge and powerful, and they eat whatever they like.

I don't really remember where I come from or how I came into this world. All I know is that I've been living in NYUAD for as long as I remember.

My most recent hobby is studying how to create websites with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I hope you like the effects I added!
